salar jung museum

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa, on the southern bank of the Musi River
in the city of hyderabad, Telangana, India.
It is one of the notable National Museums of India.[1] Originally a private art collection of the Salar Jung family, it was endowed to the nation after the death of Salar Jung III.
It was inaugurated on 16 December 1951.
